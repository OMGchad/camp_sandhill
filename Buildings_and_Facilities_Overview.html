Facility Design Philosophy

Core Design Principles

  • "Camp for Children of All Ages": Accommodates all comfort levels and group types
  • Camp-Themed Quality: Higher quality than typical camps, suitable for corporate clients
  • Florida Climate Adaptation: Insulated walls, AC/heat pumps, hurricane-resistant construction
  • Flexible Use: Buildings designed for multiple purposes and seasonal variations
  • Privacy Zones: Separate areas for different user groups to avoid interference

Construction and Development

Building Standards

  • Quality Level: Higher than typical camp facilities
  • Climate Adaptation: Insulated walls, AC/heat pumps
  • Hurricane Resistance: Florida building codes compliance
  • Corporate Appeal: Professional finishes suitable for business clients

Development Phases

  1. Phase 1: Core facilities and infrastructure
  2. Phase 2: Recreational amenities and signature features
  3. Phase 3: Expansion and enhancement opportunities

Cost Considerations

  • Construction Costs: $150-350/sq ft for buildings
  • Infrastructure: Roads, utilities, parking
  • Amenities: Sports fields, pools, trails
  • Soft Costs: Permits, design, engineering (20-30% of construction)
