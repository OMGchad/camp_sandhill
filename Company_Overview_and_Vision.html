Company Overview & Vision

Document Information

  • Version: 1.0
  • Date: 2025-01-27
  • Author: Chad Johnson
  • Assumptions: Orlando, FL market location, 150-250 acres required, Dual business model

Business Concept

Vision Statement: A premier summer camp facility and event company in the Orlando, Florida area that serves as a year-round destination for community building, adventure, and celebration in the great outdoors.

Core Business Model

The business operates as two integrated components:

1. The Venue

A comprehensive summer camp-style retreat facility designed for building community and fun in a natural setting. The venue offers:

  • Multiple accommodation levels (tent camping to luxury hotel suites)
  • Extensive recreational amenities
  • Meeting and event spaces
  • Signature 5K running trail

2. The Events Company

A full-service events company that:

  • Promotes the venue through profitable, community-focused events
  • Manages venue rentals and services
  • Creates turn-key solutions for clients
  • Develops strategic partnerships and sponsorships

Unique Value Proposition

"A Camp for Children of All Ages"

This facility bridges the gap between traditional summer camps and upscale event venues, serving multiple markets simultaneously:

Market Differentiation

  • Economic Flexibility: From tent camping ($20-35/night) to luxury hotel suites ($250-600/night)
  • Demographic Versatility: Youth groups, corporate retreats, weddings, micro-conventions, and public events
  • Geographic Advantage: "A neighbor to adventure" - proximity to Orlando's theme parks and attractions
  • Comprehensive Amenities: Unlike competitors who focus on single activities, this facility offers extensive sports, aquatics, meeting spaces, and unique features like the 5K trail

Core Values

1. Adventure-Focused (FUN!)

  • Laid-back atmosphere embracing the "Florida man" lifestyle
  • Extensive amenities for natural fun: cornhole, mini-golf, swimming, canoeing, and more
  • Focus on community bonding through shared experiences

2. Nature Promotion

  • Partnership opportunities with Orange County Parks and nature preserves
  • Public educational components similar to Oakland Nature Preserve and Tibit Nature Preserve
  • Environmental stewardship and outdoor education

3. Community Support

  • Strategic partnerships with local businesses
  • Corporate community gathering spot
  • Support for youth groups, churches, and schools
  • Local business retreats and team-building events

4. Growth-Focused

  • Continuous expansion of amenities and accommodations
  • Autonomous management of land and facilities
  • Scalable business model with multiple revenue streams

Short-Term Goals (2-3 Years)

  1. Secure Funding: Find investors or partners to realize the vision
  2. Land Acquisition: Acquire 150-250 acres in Orlando area
  3. Initial Development: Build core facilities and infrastructure
  4. Team Building: Hire key management positions
  5. Market Entry: Launch with summer camps and initial events

Long-Term Goals (10-15 Years)

  1. Family Legacy: Ensure management control remains with family
  2. Sustainable Growth: Bootstrap approach to avoid excessive debt
  3. Expansion Opportunities:
    • Additional hotel rooms (50 → 100+)
    • Enhanced convention facilities
    • Adventure activities (horseback riding, zipline, ATV, tubing)
    • Microbrewery with run club
    • Airbnb strategy for unused rooms
    • Tiny home cottages for getaways

Strategic Partnerships Vision

Multi-Year Contracts

  • Corporate partnerships (e.g., SeaWorld employee 5K with 3-year contract)
  • Title sponsorships for conventions (e.g., "Trading Card Con presented by Layton Sports Cards")
  • School partnerships for recurring camp programs

Community Integration

  • Local business retreats (WESH 2 News, AdventHealth, Track Shack)
  • Public-private partnerships for land grants or construction funding
  • Nature preserve partnerships for educational components

Competitive Advantages

  1. Market Gap: No comparable facility exists in Orlando market
  2. Flexibility: Accommodates all comfort levels and group sizes
  3. Location: Proximity to major Orlando attractions
  4. Comprehensive Amenities: All-in-one destination vs. specialized competitors
  5. Year-Round Operations: Not limited to seasonal camp activities
  6. Signature Features: 5K trail, microbrewery, unique event spaces

Risk Mitigation

  • Conservative financial approach to avoid debt liquidation
  • Diversified revenue streams across multiple markets
  • Strong community partnerships for stability
  • Flexible facility design for multiple use cases
  • Professional management structure to ensure operational excellence
