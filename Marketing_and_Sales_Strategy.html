Marketing Philosophy

Core Marketing Principles

  • Community-First: Build relationships with local businesses and organizations
  • Experience-Driven: Focus on the unique outdoor adventure experience
  • Partnership-Based: Leverage strategic partnerships for credibility and reach
  • Multi-Channel: Utilize various channels to reach different market segments

Brand Positioning

Positioning as Orlando's premier outdoor destination while leveraging proximity to theme parks and attractions.

Sales Process and Structure

Sales Team Structure

  • Corporate Sales: Dedicated account manager for business clients
  • Wedding Sales: Specialized wedding coordinator and sales team
  • Youth Programs: Camp director and sports coordinator
  • Community Events: Events director and community liaison

Sales Process

  1. Lead Generation: Marketing campaigns and referrals
  2. Qualification: Needs assessment and budget verification
  3. Presentation: Facility tours and proposal development
  4. Negotiation: Pricing and contract terms
  5. Closing: Contract signing and deposit collection
  6. Follow-up: Event execution and relationship maintenance

Pricing Strategy

  • Dynamic Pricing: Seasonal and demand-based adjustments
  • Package Deals: Bundled services for cost savings
  • Volume Discounts: Reduced rates for large groups
  • Long-term Contracts: Preferred pricing for multi-year agreements

Competitive Differentiation

Unique Selling Propositions

  • Comprehensive Amenities: All-in-one destination vs. specialized competitors
  • Flexible Accommodations: From camping to luxury hotel rooms
  • Year-Round Operations: Not limited to seasonal activities
  • Community Focus: Local business support and partnerships
  • Signature Features: 5K trail, microbrewery, unique event spaces

Competitive Advantages

  • Market Gap: No comparable facility in Orlando market
  • Location: Proximity to major attractions and business centers
  • Flexibility: Accommodates all group sizes and budgets
  • Experience: Authentic outdoor adventure with professional amenities
